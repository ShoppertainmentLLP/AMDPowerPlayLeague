Scope

Creation of a public-facing marketing landing page introducing the AMD Power Play League platform to AMD partners. The landing page communicates the value proposition, showcases the four-pillar story, previews what's inside the platform, and drives partners into the pilot sign-up flow. Includes content creation (copy, messaging, value narrative) and section design.

Deliverables

  • Public-facing single-page landing site — responsive, brand-aligned with AMD's partner identity.
  • Content creation — headline, value proposition, sub-headlines, body copy, FAQ answers, and all on-page messaging crafted for the partner audience.
  • Section design across the four-pillar narrative below, including hero, live activity feed, content preview, leaderboard preview, AI coach showcase, reward catalogue preview, and FAQ.
  • Sign-in flow — landing page CTAs wired to the portal's authentication module (the closed-group pilot sign-in).
  • One round of revisions on copy and design following AMD's review.

Key Assumptions & Dependencies

  • AMD provides branding guidelines (logos, colours, typography) at kick-off.
  • AMD confirms final approved positioning for product names, value claims, and any regulated terminology before publication.
  • AMD confirms partner audience messaging tone and any market-specific (e.g. India) localisation needs at the briefing stage.
  • Page is delivered in parallel with portal development; sign-in CTA depends on the portal authentication module being available.
  • One consolidated round of feedback / revision is included; further rounds are out of scope.

Timeline

Delivered in parallel with the Phase 1 portal — targeted to go live alongside the portal at the end of the 5-week Phase 1 window.
