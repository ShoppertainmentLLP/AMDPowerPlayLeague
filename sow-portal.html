Scope

Development and deployment of the AMD Power Play League pilot portal — a facilitator and engagement layer that sits on top of AMD's AMD Arena content. The portal curates AMD Arena training content, applies a gamification layer, and uses AI to help resellers find what they need quickly. Delivered as a pilot with self-registration via a private sign-up link distributed by AMD.

Deliverables

  • AMD Arena content integration — curated AMD Arena courses, Master Training Team meetings, and AMD-hosted webinar links surfaced as role-based learning paths.
  • Conversational AI course navigator — trained on the supplied AMD Arena catalogue and webinar data; deep-links users to relevant AMD Arena content.
  • AI certificate validation & automatic points award — AI review of uploaded completion certificates with admin escalation for flagged cases.
  • Global & regional leaderboard — live with pilot participant data; ranking by validated activity.
  • Reward shop in preview state — Partner Growth Catalogue browsable but non-transactional in Phase 1 (live redemption in Phase 3).
  • Platform-managed authentication — standalone registration & login module (interim, ahead of AMD Arena SSO).
  • Self-registration via private pilot sign-up link — audience controlled by AMD through link distribution; no admin-managed email allowlist on the platform.
  • Pilot administration console — for managing users, reviewing flagged certificates, and monitoring engagement.
  • Cloud-hosted deployment on the agreed infrastructure (AMD EPYC recommended).

Key Assumptions & Dependencies

  • AMD provides the complete AMD Arena course catalogue, webinar data, and Master Training Team meeting details in a usable, structured format.
  • AMD conducts and hosts all webinars; Power Play League surfaces them via registration / replay deep-links only.
  • AMD distributes the private pilot sign-up link to the intended pilot audience; self-registration is handled by the platform.
  • Sample completion certificates and integration access (APIs / feeds / deep-link structures) for AMD Arena are provided.
  • Branding guidelines provided at kick-off.

Timeline

5 weeks from project confirmation, conditional on dependencies being met at kick-off. Delays in providing the catalogue, participant IDs, or integration access will extend the timeline accordingly.
