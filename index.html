Frequently asked

Got questions? Straight answers.

Quick answers to what every partner asks before signing in.

Q01 Who runs Power Play League?
AMD Power Play League is an AMD India partner programme — built to help you get more out of AMD Arena, sell more partner products, and earn rewards along the way.
Q02 Do I need to make a new account?
No. You log in with your existing AMD Arena account. One sign-in for both.
Q03 Do I earn points for AMD Arena courses I take outside Power Play League?
No. You can still take the course, but you won’t earn Points. To earn Points, take the course through Power Play League.
Q04 Do I earn points if I do the same activity twice?
No. You earn Points only once per activity. You can still do it again — you just won’t get extra Points.
Q05 What happens to my points when the sprint ends?
Your Points carry over to the next sprint. They never expire. Your leaderboard rank starts fresh each quarter, but the Points you’ve earned stay yours. Swap them for rewards anytime — even after the sprint ends.
Q06 Does Power Play League only have courses?
No. Power Play League has courses, sales tools, marketing tools, ready-made answers to common customer questions, and a leaderboard with real rewards. We add new content all the time.
Q07 What if I’m not good with computers?
Power Play League is built to be simple. Your AI Assistant guides you in plain language — just tell it what you need. No tech skills required.
