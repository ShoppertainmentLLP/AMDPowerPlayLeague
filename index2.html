Got questions?

The straight answers.

If you don’t see your question here, ask the AI Coach — or write to your AMD partner manager.

Power Play League is open to all AMD partners in India. You’ll need a valid AMD Arena account and access to the private partner sign-up link — ask your AMD partner manager if you don’t have it yet.

None. The whole programme — trainings, accreditations, sales assets, AI coach, rewards — is provided at no cost by AMD India for partners.

No separate password. Power Play League uses your existing AMD Arena credentials. Click “Sign in with AMD Arena”, authenticate once, and you’re in.

Every action awards XP automatically: course completions, certifications, deals logged, customer onboards. XP accumulates across the quarter and unlocks reward tiers (5 tiers, ₹50 to ₹2,500 value). Redeem any time from the Partner Growth Catalogue. Quarterly sprint resets the leaderboard but not your accumulated rewards.

Most rewards are cash-equivalent and delivered as digital gift cards, vouchers, or direct shipping. Tax treatment depends on your jurisdiction — please check with your accountant. We provide a redemption history for your records.

It surfaces the right training, the right asset, and the right answer at the right time. Examples: “what should I learn next?”, “draft an objection response to X”, “what’s in my pipeline for Ryzen AI?”. Trained on AMD’s own content. Conversations stay within your partner workspace.

The leaderboard shows partner names + organisations + XP for top 100 each quarter (with consent). You can opt out of public ranking and still earn rewards. Your activities, customer data, and pipeline are never shared publicly — just aggregate XP.

Median path is roughly one quarter for partners who actively engage — complete 5-8 courses, log a handful of deals, and use the assets/playbooks. Top 10 each quarter earn the Champion designation.

Whole team. Each team member needs their own AMD Arena account and access to the partner link. Each person earns their own XP and rewards. Aggregate team performance is also tracked for organisation-level recognition.

Three places: (1) Ask the AI Coach — it handles 80% of questions. (2) Your AMD partner manager. (3) The in-platform help link, which routes to a Power Play League ops mailbox monitored business hours AEST.
