The Gaps Power Play League Will Close
Where AMD Arena & AMD Partner Portal Fall Short
AMD has the content. The gap is in how partners discover it, engage with it, and are rewarded for using it. The Power Play League is built to close exactly these gaps.
AMD Arena
Training & certification platform
Overwhelming, unorganised catalogue
Hundreds of videos with no clear paths. Partners don't know what to watch — so they don't watch anything.
No gamification or healthy competition
Watching a course feels solitary. No ranks, no streaks, no peer benchmarks — none of what drives engagement on modern platforms.
No rewards for certifications
A reseller who completes an Expert certification gets the same recognition as one who watched a single video — zero. Effort isn't tied to outcome.
No AI guidance
No personalised recommendations, no smart summaries, no conversational search. Partners are on their own to figure out what's relevant.
AMD Partner Portal
Sales & marketing collateral hub
Asset library is hard to traverse
Hundreds of files across products, regions, and formats. Resellers can't find what they need — and when they do, they're not sure which version to use.
No AI for discovery or content creation
No conversational search to find the right asset, no auto-personalisation of templates, no localisation, no campaign bundles. Every asset is hand-picked, manually.
No engagement or proof-of-execution loop
Downloads aren't tracked back to outcomes. AMD has no signal on which assets actually drove a campaign, a deal, or a customer conversation.
